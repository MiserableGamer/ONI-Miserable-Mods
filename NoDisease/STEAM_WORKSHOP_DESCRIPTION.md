[h1]No Disease[/h1]

Completely disables the disease system - not just immunity, but removes disease mechanics entirely from the game.

[b][i]Note: This mod is intended as an interim solution until [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1967921388]FastTrack[/url] by Peter Han is updated for the current game version.[/i][/b]

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]No Germs[/b] - All existing germs are wiped from the world on load
[*][b]No Disease Spread[/b] - Germs cannot spread through air, water, or pipes
[*][b]No Infections[/b] - Duplicants cannot get sick from germs
[*][b]No Disease UI[/b] - Disease overlay and panels are hidden
[*][b]No Disease Tools[/b] - Disinfect tool is removed
[*][b]Hidden Buildings[/b] - Disease-related buildings are deprecated
[/list]

[h2]How to Use[/h2]
[olist]
[*][b]Install the Mod[/b] - Enable in the Mods menu
[*][b]Load Your Save[/b] - Existing germs will be removed automatically
[*][b]Play Disease-Free[/b] - No further action needed
[/olist]

[h3]Tips[/h3]
[list]
[*]This is an interim solution while FastTrack is updated
[*]Apothecary is kept when Radiation DLC is enabled (needed for rad pills)
[*]Great for players who find disease tedious or want better performance
[*]Safe to add to existing saves - germs are wiped on load
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Oxygen Not Included[/b] - Build 700386 or later
[*][b]Mod API[/b] - Version 2
[*][b]DLC Support[/b] - Works with base game and all DLC
[*][b]Other Mods[/b] - Compatible with most mods
[/list]

[h2]Performance[/h2]
[b]Improves Performance[/b]
[list]
[*][b]No Disease Simulation[/b] - Removes germ calculations entirely
[*][b]No Spread Processing[/b] - No need to calculate disease transmission
[*][b]Reduced Memory Usage[/b] - Disease containers not needed
[/list]
