# Discord Server Rules

## üìã General Rules

### 1. Be Respectful
- Treat all members with kindness and respect
- No harassment, bullying, or hate speech of any kind
- Disagreements are fine, but keep discussions civil
- Personal attacks will not be tolerated

### 2. Keep It On Topic
- This server is focused on Oxygen Not Included modding
- Off-topic discussions should be kept to designated channels
- Spam, self-promotion, or advertising unrelated content is not allowed

### 3. No NSFW Content
- Keep all content appropriate for all ages
- No explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate material
- This includes usernames, avatars, and shared content

### 4. Use Appropriate Channels
- Post questions, bugs, and feature requests in the correct channels
- Use GitHub for detailed bug reports and feature requests
- Keep general discussions in the appropriate discussion channels

### 5. Search Before Asking
- Check if your question has been answered before
- Look through GitHub issues and discussions
- Use the search function to find relevant information

### 6. Provide Context
- When asking for help, provide relevant information:
  - Game version and mod versions
  - Steps to reproduce issues
  - Screenshots or logs when applicable
  - What you've already tried

### 7. Respect Intellectual Property
- Don't share copyrighted content without permission
- Give credit where credit is due
- Don't claim others' work as your own

### 8. No Piracy or Illegal Content
- Discussion of piracy, cracks, or illegal downloads is prohibited
- Sharing or requesting pirated content will result in immediate action

### 9. Follow Discord Terms of Service
- You must be 13+ to use Discord (and this server)
- Follow all Discord Community Guidelines
- Violations may result in reporting to Discord

### 10. Moderation Decisions
- Moderators have final say in rule interpretations
- If you have concerns about moderation, contact a moderator privately
- Repeated rule violations may result in warnings, mutes, or bans
